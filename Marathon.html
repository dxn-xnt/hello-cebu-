Cebu City Marathon: A Celebration of Fitness and Fun

About the event

The Cebu City Marathon is a premier running event in the Philippines, coinciding with the Sinulog Festival. It attracts both local and international runners, offering a festive and scenic route through Cebu City's streets. The marathon features several categories, including 5K, 10K, 21K (half marathon), and 42K (full marathon). Known for its well-organized setup and lively atmosphere, the event often includes hydration stations, entertainment along the route, and cheer squads to motivate participants. It’s more than just a race—it’s a celebration of health, fitness, and community spirit, set against the backdrop of Sinulog festivities.

Time and Date:

The Cebu City Marathon is held annually on the second Sunday of January, starting early in the morning, typically around 3:00 AM to 4:00 AM.

Location:

The race begins and ends at IT Park, Cebu City, with routes that pass through key landmarks and thoroughfares, giving runners a unique way to experience the city's vibrant culture and architecture.
