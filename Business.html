Cebu Business Month: Where Commerce Meets Celebration

About the event

Cebu Business Month, spearheaded by the Cebu Chamber of Commerce and Industry (CCCI), is an annual celebration that promotes business growth and economic development in Cebu. The event serves as a platform for networking, collaboration, and showcasing innovation across various industries, including information technology, tourism, entrepreneurship, and creative sectors. Activities include business summits, trade fairs, expos, seminars, and workshops aimed at fostering partnerships and addressing emerging challenges in the business landscape. CBM also emphasizes sustainability and global competitiveness, making it a significant event for entrepreneurs and industry leaders.

Time and Date:

Cebu Business Month (CBM) is celebrated throughout the entire month of June, with activities and events scheduled on various dates within the month.

Location:

CBM events are held in Cebu City and nearby areas, with key venues including the Cebu International Convention Center, SM Seaside City Cebu, and Ayala Center Cebu.
