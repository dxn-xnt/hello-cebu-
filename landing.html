Cebu City

Cebu City, the "Queen City of the South," is the oldest city in the Philippines and a vibrant hub for trade, tourism, and culture. Known for landmarks like Magellan’s Cross and the Basilica Minore del Santo Niño, it blends rich history with modern urban life. Highlighted by the grand Sinulog Festival and famous for its delicious lechon, Cebu City is a top destination and a dynamic center of growth.
